Basics of Crocheting

Single Stitch

Step 1:

Once your chain is made, add one extra loop.

Step 2:

Insert the hook into the second chain from the hook.

Step 3:

Wrap the yarn around the hook and pull the yarn through the FIRST loop on the hook, after that, wrap the yarn around the hook again and pull it through BOTH. Once this is done, insert the hook into the very next chain.

Step 4:

Repeat this process all the way across the chain.

Step 5:

Add one chain up, then repeat this process all the way across to add a second row.
