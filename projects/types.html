Types of Prayer Shawls

The Shawl worn by the Jewish Men

These shawls are a garment, typically made of wool or cotton, that was symbolizes nobility.

Prayer Shawls that are Gifted

These types of prayer shawls are handmade by crocheting to knitting. They are made with intention and prayed over then gifted to someone going through adversity. For example, medical issues, lost, etc.
