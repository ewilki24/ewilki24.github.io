Basics of Crocheting

Half-Double Stitch

Step 1:

Once your chain is made, add two more chain loops.

Step 2:

Wrap yarn around the hook and then insert the hook into the third chain form the hook.

Step 3:

Wrap yarn around the hook and pull it through the FIRST loop on the hook, there should be three loops on the hook. After that wrap the yarn around the hook again and pull it through the last THREE hooks, there should only be one loop on the hook.

Step 4:

Repeat this process in the very next chain loop and do this across the row.

Step 5:

Once the row is completed, chain up two and repeat the process to make the second row.
