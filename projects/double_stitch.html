Basics of Crocheting

Double Stitch

Step 1:

Once your chain is made, add three chain loops.

Step 2:

Wrap the yarn around the hook once and insert the hook into the fourth chain from the hook.

Step 3:

There should be three loops on the hook. Wrap yarn around the hook and pull it throught the FIRST two loops on the hook, there should now be two loops on the hook. Wrap yarn around the hook again and pull it throught the LAST two loops on the hook, there should only be one loop on the hook.

Step 4:

Repeat the process in the next chain and repeat until you complete the chain.

Step 5:

Once you complete the row, chain up three and repeat the process to make the second row.
