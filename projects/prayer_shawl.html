What is a Prayer Shawl?

History of the Prayer Shawl

Prayer Shawls are typically worn by Jewish men while attending synagogue. They wear them over their heads during prayer.

The Bible does not require prayer shawls, however, it requires a specific tassels which they make into prayer shawls.

This requirement is acknowledged is Deuteronmony 22:12 NRSV You shall make tassels on the four corners of the cloak with which you cover yourself.

An important thing to note is that prayer shawls are not required by Christian faith, it's an individual decision.
(Source: JustDisciple)

What is the shawl MMC is making?

These Prayer Shawls are handmade shawls that were made by crocheting. They can be made by knitting, however this groups primarily makes them from crocheting.

The process of making them is with intention, as it is infused with prayer, typically with prayer of healing.
