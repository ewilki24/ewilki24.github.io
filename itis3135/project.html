Project Overview

  • Purpose: The purpose of this website is to inform people about prayer shawls and how they are made and can get involved.
  • Intended Users: This site's intended audience is people interested in learning prayer shawls and/or how they are made if they want to get involved in that process.
  • Overall: Overall this content is more of an informative situation about a church group in my hometown who are learning to make prayer shawls via crocheting and how other people can get involved in doing the same. I was specifically asked to not provide an ordering application.
