Where

Where can you learn to Crochet?

There are various different resources you can use to learn how to crochet. Whether it be a guide book, YouTube or a family member. Nowadays people are most likely to use YouTube however you can learn from anywhere.

Where can you Crochet?

Realistically, you can crochet from just about anywhere. It's very easy to travel with and is something that can keep you busy when there's downtime.

Where do you get the supplies to Crochet?

You can get the supplies from just about any store that has arts and crafts in it. It's important to note that when you buy the supplies, the hook you use is dependent the size of the yarn.

Getting Started

Once that is done, you pull the knot close, then wrap yarn around the hook and pull it through the loop. Repeat this process until you make a chain like shown.

Treble Stitch

This stitch its probably my favorite one to do. Its one of the looser stitches allows me to work faster.

To do this, you want to wrap the yarn around the hook twice than insert the hook into the chain.

Once that is done you want to wrap yarn around the hook and pull it through the FIRST two loops on the hook. Repeat this process until you are down to one loop on the hook.

Why

Why did I begin to Crochet?

As a kid I was very crafty and always trying to learn new projects. I grew up watching my Granny make Prayer Shawls and my Grandma making hats for infants at the hospital. I wanted to try it out and eventually it became a way to occupy myself without being on a device. Since I spend so much time on devices like my laptop and phone, it's nice having something to do without looking at a computer screen.

Why might others Crochet?

Others may crochet for the same reason. Other people that I know say that it's a great stress reliever and it's also become a great way to bond with family members who have recently learned to crochet.

Is it hard to learn?

Depending on the person, it can be pretty difficult to learn. It takes a lot of patience and practice but once you get the hang of it, it becomes second nature.
