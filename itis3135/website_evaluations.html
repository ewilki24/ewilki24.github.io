Emily Wilkinson || ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

Good Website

This is the main homepage of the History Channel website that consist of recent episodes that have aired and shows produced by "History".

  • Contrast: No color clashes too much and everything stands out as it should.
  • Repetition: Everything is consistent throughout that I noticed from fonts to visuals.
  • Alignment: Website was strategically aligned to provide as much content as possible, but clear as possible.
  • Proximity: Content is grouped together based on new episodes and upcoming episodes.

Bad Website

This seems to be a foreign website that sells a lot of machinery like ATV's, Go-Carts, and more.

  • Contrast: Many different colors, some things stand out but others clash with some random highlights throughout the page.
  • Repetition: There are repeated elements but the only consistent thing is the font on the page.
  • Alignment: There is little to no alignment on the page, evrything is just randomly placed.
  • Proximity: Everything seems to be sectioned off but within that, it is incredibly placed randomly. What I mean by this is what is being "sold" isn't grouped by a category.
